The Other Worlds Zine Fair is an annual zine fair in Sydney (Australia) on the last Sunday of May. Now in its 7th year, it attracts over 100 stall holders and 200 attendees.

The zine fair was born out of the 2014 Sydney Biennale boycotts. These boycotts formed in protest against Australia’s policy of mandatory detention of refugees and asylum seekers.

Applying for a table

Applications for the 2021 Zine Fair open in January 2021. Follow our Facebook page for updates and news.

The online fair

In 2020, because of COVID-19, we replaced our in-person fair with a virtual one. You can visit the fair here. Make sure you don’t miss Plastic Rupert’s 5 hour concert in the virtual bathrooms!
