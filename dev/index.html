Other Worlds is a zine collective based in Australia. Every May we hold an annual zine fair in Sydney. We also run quarterly postal subscription service.

Other Worlds has been collectively organised since 2014. We are a progressive organisation that hopes to provide a space for marginalised voices. We acknowledge that Other Worlds takes place on the stolen lands of the Gadigal and Wangal people of the Darug nation.

Subscription service

The Other Worlds Subscription Quaterly Annual Mailout (SQAM) is a zine subscription service. Every three months we post a curated pack of zines loosely collected around a theme. There will be four packs in total.

The first pack drops in August 2020 and is titled “The City as Might Have Been”.

Subscriptions are currently open and limited to 50 subscribers.

The Other Worlds Zine Fair

On the last Sunday of May we hold the Other Worlds Zine Fair in Sydney, Australia. Now in its 7th year, it attracts over 100 stall holders and 200 attendees.

The zine fair was born out of the 2014 Biennale boycotts of Transfield (the service providers for Australia’s offshore immigration detention centres).
